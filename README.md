# Elevvo-Pathways-Internship-NLPP
The Elevvo Pathways Natural Language Processing (NLP) program is a structured, project-based internship designed to bridge the gap between academic theory and industry application. It is primarily remote and offers a flexible environment for learners ranging from beginners to early professionals.

Here is a breakdown of what the program typically entails:

🌟 Program Overview
Duration: Flexible tracks of 2 weeks (intensive) or 1 month (full experience).

Format: 100% remote and self-paced, allowing students and working professionals to participate around their existing schedules.

Cost: Often offers a Fully Sponsored (Free) tier for standard participation, with a "Premium" tier for additional mentorship and faster certification.

📚 Curriculum & Learning Path
The NLP track focuses on the "Practical Execution" of language models. Rather than just watching videos, you work through a series of tasks that mirror professional engineering workflows.

Foundation: Introduction to Python for Data Science (NumPy, Pandas).

Preprocessing: Cleaning unstructured text data, tokenization, and vectorization.

Core NLP Tasks:

Sentiment Analysis: Building classifiers to determine the "mood" of a text.

Text Classification: Categorizing documents or emails automatically.

NER (Named Entity Recognition): Identifying names, dates, and locations in text.

Advanced Topics: Exploring Deep Learning for text using TensorFlow or Keras, and an introduction to Large Language Model (LLM) concepts.
